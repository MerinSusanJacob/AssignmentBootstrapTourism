Idukki

A landlocked district, Idukki is one of the most nature rich areas of Kerala. High ranges and wooded valleys are girded by three main rivers – Periyar, Thalayar and Thodupuzhayar – and their tributaries. The River Pamba also has its origin here. As a tourist destination, Idukki offers diverse attractions like Wildlife sancturies, Hill stations, Dams, Spice plantation tours, Mountain treks, Elephant rides etc.

Top tourist destinations in Idukki

Periyar Tiger Reserve

Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary is one of the best places in India to experience nature and wildlife in close quarters

Meesapulimala

The second highest peak in the Western Ghats after Anamudi, Meesapulimala in the Idukki district is a popular spot for trekking and camping

Munnar

Munnar, a popular hill station located in Idukki district, Kerala, India.
