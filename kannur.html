Kannur

Kannur is a coastal city in the south Indian state of Kerala. It was once an ancient trading port. Enduring monuments such as 16th-century St. Angelo Fort, once occupied by European colonial forces, show the cityâ€™s significant role in the spice trade.

Top tourist destinations in Kannur

Muzhappilangad beach

Muzhuppilangad Beach gathers its fame as the only drive-in beach in India as well as in Asia. Lying 8 km away from Thalassery and 16 km away from Kannur town, the beach is easily accessible by road.

Fort St. Angelo

Fort St. Angelo, popularly known as Kannur Kotta is located at a distance of 3 km from the town of Kannur. The fort offers excellent vista of the surrounding regions as it lies facing the roaring waves of the Arabian Sea.

Paithalmala

Pythal Mala, also known as Vaital Mala, is a scenic hill station located on the Kerala-Karnataka border and can be reached by travelling about 60 km from Kannur town.
