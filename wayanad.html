Wayanad

Lying amidst the majestic western ghats, Wayanad is a famous tourist destination that quenches the thirst of all kinds of travelers with its evergreen landscapes and blissful weather. Offering a mind-blowing treat to solo travelers, honeymooners, and families with children, this small hamlet is sure to win over your heart with its awe-inspiring natural beauty. A place where you can see what nature looks like. A place that is no less than a little paradise in reality.

Top tourist destinations in Wayanad

Banasura Sagar dam

Boating in the lake is a hit activity among the adventure seekers there and you can also give a shot to horse riding, camel riding, and trekking to enjoy your visit to the fullest.

Soochipara Falls

Ideal for rock climbing, the crystal-clear waters falling from Soochipara Falls join the Chaliyar River and form a pool where you can sit back, relax, dip your feet, and chill for hours.

Kuruva Island

Situated amidst the Kabini River, the Kuruva Island is a lovely paradise that allows people of all ages to please their minds and soothe their inner souls in the most beautiful surroundings.
